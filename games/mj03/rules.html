Ma-Jong Rules

These are the Ma-Jong rules played by at least one family in Shanghai.  Add up the points for each matching rule to get your point score.  Rules that cause score doubling are listed below the various point rules.

Definitions:

'po / 碰牌' means that you claim a piece as it is being discarded to make a set of 3 identical pieces.  You can 'po' from any player, even if it would not normally have been your turn to play next. The first two pieces of the set of 3 must come from the player's hand.

'cha / 吃' means that you claim a piece as it is being discarded to make a straight of 3.  You can only 'cha' from the player to your right since you must follow the normal order of each player's turn.  This assumes that players take turns in a clockwise order.  Two pieces of the straight of 3 must come from the player's hand.

'gahng / 扛' means that you are making a set of 4 identical pieces.  This can be done by claiming a piece as it is being discarded by any player, just as with a 'po', and all 'po' rules apply will also apply.  It can also be done by drawing a piece from the deck as would normally be done at the start of each player's turn.   The first three pieces of the set of 4 must come from the player's hand.  To compensate for the 4th piece in the set, the player draws an extra piece from the flower-end of the deck.  If the 4th piece was drawn normally, and not from another player's discarded piece ('po'), then the set of 4 must be played face-down so that others cannot see which set of 4 has been played from the player's hand.  For scoring purposes, a set of 4 can always count as a set of 3.

'zzzmo / 自摸' means that you got the winning piece by drawing it yourself, not by claiming a piece as it was being discarded by another player.

'ztail / 槓上開花' is the same as zzzmo, except that the winning tile was drawn from the flower pile, which would happen when drawing a replacement tile after playing a flower or creating a gahng.

'words' are the pieces that don't have a number associated with them.  These include:

  • the 4 winds (east, south, west, north), and
  • the 3 non-wind words: fa (prosperity), middle, and babei (nothing)

 

Points Description
+1
  1. each flower
  2. a pair of 2, 5 or 8.  Does not apply with 7-pair win
  3. 'sisters': each pair of matching straights of different suits
  4. no words and no flowers
  5. no 1's and no 9's
  6. no 5's
  7. each set of 3 non-wind words
  8. no 'po' or 'cha', except on last (winning) piece
  9. have a 5 from each suit
  10. all straights
  11. waiting-for-1: win was only possible with a single piece
  12. each missing suit
  13. set of 3 of the current wind
  14. each 'gahng' (set of 4)
+2
  1. no 'po' or 'cha', including the last (winning) piece
  2. identical sisters: each pair of matching straights of the same suit
  3. young & old: (1, 2, 3) + (7, 8, 9) of the same suit
+3
  1. All different pieces, with no numbered pieces closer than 3 to any other numbered piece of the same suit.  Only applies if the more restricted +7 point 'all different' rule is not met
  2. mixed dragon: 3 straights of 3 different suits forming the sequence 1 .. 9
  3. 2 sets of 3 non-wind words + a pair of the other non-wind words
+5
  1. dragon: 3 straights of the same suit forming the sequence 1 .. 9
  2. only sets of 3 (no straights).  Note: gahng sets of 4 score like sets of 3, so gahng is always OK.
  3. all 'po' or 'cha': all sets/straights and the pair include a piece discarded by other players
  4. all 5 suits represented: includes 3 numbered suits, a wind, and a non-wind word
  5. all same numbered suit + words
  6. have 1 or 9 in each numbered suit set or straight.  Words OK
+7
  1. All different pieces, with one of the two following extra conditions satisfied:
    • all words present, or
    • 9 numbered pieces, with each numbered piece being exactly 3 away from other pieces in the same suit.
      This requires that numbered suits follow one of these possible patterns: (1, 4, 7), (2, 5, 8), or (3, 6, 9)
+10
  1. all same suit, no words
  2. every set consists of only 1, 9, or words
+20
  1. 7 pairs
  2. 3 sets of 3 non-wind words
  3. no other points (multipliers still apply)
Double
  1. 'zzzmo / 自摸', get the last (winning) piece yourself.  If winning piece came from the flower pile, double again (×4 / ztail / 槓上開花).
  2. dealer always pays/collects double
  3. 3rd-time dealer, stake doubles again.  Repeat each deal of current dealer.  3rd deal is x4, 4th deal is x8, 5th deal is x16, ...

Special play-time scoring:

+10
  1. If any single piece is discarded by 4 consecutive players, the player who first discarded the piece must immediately pay each other player 10 points. The game server does not implement this rule.
