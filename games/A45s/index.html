About

Growing up in NB, I enjoyed playing Auction 45s with family and friends. I created this online game purely as a hobby project, to allow fans of this game to play together, even when they are far apart. When playing with friends, establish a call using apps such as Hangouts, Skype, Messenger, or Zoom, then have everyone join a game together. Almost like old times.
