Auction 45s help

Entering Play

Pressing play_arrowPlay  will take you to the play table login screen. You can select any username. No registration is currently required.

The Play Lobby

To play a solo game against 3 robots, sit at any of the seats in an open game. Empty public games are created as needed, so there will always be one available.

Press the play_circle_outline Start button to deal the first hand. When starting a new game series, the first dealer will be the 'East' player.

When you are playing with friends, you can choose to create an invitational game. Your invitational game will only be visible to the players you invite. Pressing the Invite button will allow you to select from currently connected players, as well as players that you've invited to any of your past games.

The Play Table

Game play will start with bidding. The highest bidder with then get to browse cards from the kitty, and select the trump suit. Five rounds of trick play concludes a hand. See the game rules for more details.

Past Tricks

During trick play, the cards played in completed tricks can be viewed by clicking on any of the piles of cards in the Past Tricks area. Past tricks are oriented vertically or horizontally to align with the team that won that trick.

Score History

The score history button, assignment, is in the upper left corner of the play table, between the game scores for the white team and the red team.

Lobby Button

While at the play table, you can temporarily return to the lobby by pressing the  Lobby  button in the upper right corner of the play table. This can be useful to invite additional players to your game. Return to the play table by clicking in the green play table area, or swipe the lobby panel left on mobile devices.
