Teriyaki Steak

2 cloves garlic

¼ c. soya sauce

¼ c. pineapple juice

2 tbsp. oil

1 tbsp. lemon juice or vinegar

2-3 tbsp. brown sugar

1 tbsp. ketchup, if desired

¼ tsp. ginger

¼ tsp. paprika

2 lb London Broil (flank steak, or any steak)

Mince garlic, and mix with all remaining ingredients except the meat.

Pour over meat. Marinate 1 to 2 hours at room temperature, turning meat once, or 1 to 3 days in refrigerator, covered, for thicker cuts of meat. Remove meat from marinade and broil, barbeque, or pan fry.
