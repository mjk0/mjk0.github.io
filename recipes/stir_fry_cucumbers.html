Stir-Fried Cucumbers

6 persian cucumbers

½ carrot, round slices

¼ red pepper, Julienne cut

3 cloves garlic, sliced

1 tsp. yellow bean paste, spicy

Cut cucumbers, with skin, into medium slices. Marinate with 1 tsp. salt for 15 minutes. Use cheese cloth to squeeze out water as much as possible

In wok or pan, heat oil, put in garlic slices. Add bean paste. Add cucumbers and carrots. Stir fry for a while, then add red peppers. Add dashi powder and a little light soy sauce. No need to add salt because of salt in cucumbers.
